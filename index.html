Other than having a passion in IT, she finds herself as a people person who enjoys interacting with people. This actually teaches her to develop her interpersonal and communication skills.

Fortunately, she is still a normal and sweet lady who holds feminine characteristics. Also, she is a brand-conscious type of person but isn’t a shopaholic. She likes white, black or white and black colours yet she has always wanted to live in a colourful world.

Growing up as the third from all four daughters of a lovely couple, she understands that there is always a door of improvement to everyone. She is an epistemophilic individual who never stops working on self-betterment. During her spare time, she normally watches and listens to motivational videos as a lullaby. Be it videos about tech to keep up with the latest news, videos about the English language to improve her English, videos about relationships, videos about skincare as well as videos from Islamic scholars to become spiritually strong. Also, she likes reading about financial management as she is struggling hard towards financial freedom. Indeed, she will always try her very best version of Nur Afeeqah to stay upbeat and positive in all possible ways.

Famous as Peqa from most of her friends and acquaintances, Peqa might look like an adult who is trapped in a kid’s body as she is physically small, but she has a BIG heart. She is a selfless lady who always puts others before herself. Practising altruistic acts is her natural inner beauty but at the same time, she tries learning to be shrewd enough to love herself even more because at the end of the day, she is the only one who is responsible for her own happiness. No matter what, being selfish isn't her forte.

Ok, she is now done bragging and flexing humbly about her life, but the truth, she is far away from perfect that she cannot let anyone know, just let her keep to herself and focusing on what is good to be seen.
